About

Hi, my name is Sara Chabot. I'm a rising senior at James Madison University studying Interactive Design and Music Industry. I'm originally from Okinawa, Japan, and I moved to the United States 5 years ago to pursue a college education. Over the last year, I've created logos, yardsigns, human size silouhette using Adobe Indesign, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator. I've also been studying and conducting User Research and created websites as well as designed app pages for my coursework. This summer, I had the opportunity to work as a design intern at Stable and Wise and helped create and manage their web content and designed two of their E-books. My goal is to work in the multimedia content creator and user research designer in the Music Industry.
